BLOOD GLUCOSE CURVE WORKSHEET

Thank you for taking the time to perform this blood glucose curve at home.

This will help your veterinary team see a more accurate representation of your pet's blood glucose without the stress of visiting the hospital.

Instructions:

  • Take a blood glucose measurement before feeding your pet and record the value and time in the worksheet.
  • Feed your pet the typical amount.
  • Administer insulin as directed by your veterinarian.
  • Obtain blood glucose readings as directed by your veterinarian.It is typical to get a reading every 2-4 hours but this may differ from your veterinarian's recommendation for your pet.
  • Fill in the time you took the reading and the glucose measurement.
  • Submit to your veterinarian for evaluation. Never make insulin adjustments without consulting your veterinarian.
